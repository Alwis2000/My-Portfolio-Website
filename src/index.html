About Me

I am an aspiring ‘creator’, hoping to reach multiple mediums of creations! I currently focus on the 2D and 3D mediums now, with a little bit of game dev on the side.

Ever since I was a child, I have always dreamt of making creations, whether it be to help people or just to put what I have to offer to this vast world. I have to thank Dreamworks, Pixar and Paramount for really inspiring my youngself to really show the capabilities of what a person can do.

The reason I have this website to showcase what I have made so far is because of my philosophy that anyone can do it regardless of "natural" talent. What I hope to achieve is to inspire anyone seeing my work, that anyone can achieve anything as long as you have the will to! considering I Learnt Blender and UE during the pandemic as a way of wasting time, from which I learnt how to make CG Creations and games, and I got to love making them.

Anyways, I hope you like what you see here in my portfolio!
